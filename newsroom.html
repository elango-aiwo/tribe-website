Boilerplate

AIWO Tribe is a measured workforce longevity programme. Over a 12-month journey, every enrolled employee receives a 200+ biomarker baseline, a physician-signed Smart Report, a personalised plan across 11+1 lifestyle pillars and the LifeOS app, re-measured on a quarterly cycle. Employers receive an aggregate, anonymised view only. Outcomes are reported as measured trajectories, never guarantees, and the programme is physician-reviewed, never "AI-diagnosed."
