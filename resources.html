Glossary

The language of measured longevity.

The hero metric: the years lived with vitality, energy and independence. One number employees understand and the board can track, computed from the standard panel and re-scored every cycle.

The measurement framework: 200+ biomarkers organised into 7 intuitive panels (Metabolic, Hormonal, Inflammatory, Longevity, Nutritional, Genomic, Organ Function), producing 8–10 organ ages.

The 90-day method: Signals → Interpretations → Interventions → Systems. A perpetual quarterly cycle that turns raw data into action and re-measures the result.

The 11+1 lifestyle pillars (Sleep, Breathing, Nutrition, Social, Exercise, Stress, Circadian, Purpose, Environmental, Gut Health, Hydration + the Genetic & Epigenetic Foundation) that turn every finding into a livable action.

The employee's physician-signed report: JoySpan Score, organ ages and the 7 panels in plain language, with one prioritised plan — released only after named physician sign-off.

8–10 organ-level biological ages (heart, liver, kidney, metabolic, immune and more), computed from one draw. "Your heart is 8 years older than you are" is the line that drives action.
