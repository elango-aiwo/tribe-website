What we stand for

Five principles, non-negotiable.

Proof over activity

We measure outcomes, not engagement theatre. If it can't be shown, we don't claim it.

Privacy by design

Single-channel, aggregate-only. The employer–employee data boundary is absolute.

Physician-led always

A named clinician signs off every report. Physician-reviewed, never "AI-diagnosed."

Hope, not fear

We lead with what's possible — joyspan grown — not with anxiety about disease.

Honest benchmarks

Trajectories, not guarantees. Comparisons attributed until your cohort has its own data.
