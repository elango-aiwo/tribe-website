The two-level model

Two views of one outcome — strictly separated.

The individual sees everything. The employer sees only the aggregate. The boundary is absolute, by design.

For the individual · private

Everything, to the employee alone

  • Full Smart Report — JoySpan Score, 8–10 organ ages, 12 scores
  • Their personalised, physician-signed plan
  • Quarterly progress, in their LifeOS app
  • Never shared with the employer — single-channel
For the enterprise · aggregate

Only the workforce picture, anonymised

  • Aggregate JoySpan & organ-age distribution
  • Year-over-year movement & engagement
  • Detection themes & ESG-legible metrics
  • Minimum-cohort suppression — no re-identification
